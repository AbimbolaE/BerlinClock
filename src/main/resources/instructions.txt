
Welcome to the Berlin Clock program

To view a Berlin Clock as ASCII art, enter a time as string of the format HH:mm:ss

For example:

    $ sbt "run 17:33:22"

will produce:

    -----------------------
    |          Y          |
    -----------------------
    |  R  | R  |  R |  B  |
    -----------------------
    |  R  | R  |  B |  B  |
    -----------------------
    |Y|Y|R|Y|Y|R|B|B|B|B|B|
    -----------------------
    |  Y  | B  |  B |  B  |
    -----------------------
